The Scheduling Algorithm

How background calculations organize your study loops

Adaptive Ebbinghaus Curves

Humans forget abstract details exponentially. MedRecall tracks recall quality after each session and mathematically stretches the next interval, keeping study workloads low while locking items in long-term memory.

Dynamic Interval Calculation

When finishing a topic, self-assess. The scheduler instantly updates the next review date using this formula:

Next Due = Today + (N × Multiplier)
Struggled

Resets multiplier. Next review falls exactly 1 day later to reinforce memory.

Hesitant

Progressive schedule growth. Multiplier is scaled safely by 1.8x.

Mastered

Rapid leap. Multiplier is accelerated by 2.6x, saving massive study hours.

Subject-Specific Exam Compression (EPC)

Each subject has a custom target exam date. If standard spaced repetition intervals push a topic's next due date past its exam, the algorithm intercepts this and compresses the review window to guarantee a vital final pass 3 days before exam morning.
